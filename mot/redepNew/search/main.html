Welcome to the
Redeployment Website

The Redeployment System consolidates all internal job opportunities in Motorola and is being made available not only to internal Motorolans but also to those affected by reductions for a limited period of time. This site replaces all other IOS systems. As positions become available through either vacancies caused by voluntary separations from Motorola or new opportunities that develop, we will make them available to you here. A cross-functional team of Motorolans is engaged in design of a new system for job postings which will be launched sometime in the latter half of 1999.

If you find a job opening that matches your interests, experiences and skills, you will have the opportunity to apply for the position on-line. After submitting five applications you will receive a warning asking you to talk to your local HR or Staffing representative. After ten applications you will no longer be allowed to use the Redeployment System until you've had a discussion with HR. Once you've applied on-line, you will receive an acknowledgement that your application has been received and it will be forwarded to the hiring manager for review. The hiring manager will then contact you directly.
