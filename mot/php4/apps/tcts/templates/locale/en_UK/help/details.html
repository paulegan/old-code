TCTS Help :: Overview :: Details


1.

Click on the employee commerce ID to show all transactions for this employee.

2.

Click on the batch ID to show all transactions in this batch.

3.

Click on the "Re-opened from" ID to view the transaction that this was opened from with the "Re-open" function.

4.

For transactions with a status of "Open" the close button will be displayed. Click this to change the status to "Closed".

5.

Click this button to delete the transaction from the system.

6.

For transactions with a status of "Closed" the "Reopen" button will be displayed. Click this to create a new Open transaction using the details of the currently displayed.

7.

To return to the search form, click on the "Search Again" button.

8.

For transactions with a status of "Pending" the "Integrate" and "Reject" buttons will be displayed. To reject first select the reject reason from the list, then click on the "Reject" button.

9.

To change the status of a "Pending" transaction to "Open" click on the "Integrate" button.
